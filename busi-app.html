5 Best Business Apps In 2021

You've probably been searching for a mobile business app to help in managing travelling expenses,collecting and processing business ideas and most importantly, bringing your products and services to a prospect. You are at the right place so no need to worry, because we are going to checkout the best business apps in 2021.

The apps below will help you share and collect files and also keep you up with your prospect.

  1. Google Workspace:

    This is an efficient business app that helps a business owner to create and share drawings business plans and other ideas that will help in improving a business. It is suitable for both PC's and smartphones. Below are three good reasons you need to use Google Workspace.
    • Launching websites on Google site without a programming experience:
      Users can create and launch websites by dragging desired content from Google workspace into templates.
    • Preservation of data:
      You can save your data in Google vault. Google vault helps both the employers and employees to preserve vital data thus preventing data loss. You can also use Google vault to check users activities.
    • Good security system:
      Google workspace allows a business owner to remove and add users and groups, assign duties and manage devices. Google workspace also comes with improved email scanning that helps checks for security threats.

  2. Evernote:

    This a business app that helps you to recall ideas you have made in the past. Here are some of the reasons Evernote has been able to make it to the top business apps in 2021.
    • It can scan texts in a picture using Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology.
    • Evernote makes life easier by enhancing working in groups so as to facilitate business activities.
    • It can be used to set reminder for various activities in order to let you keep up to time.
    • It allows you to attach pictures and audio to a note right from the app.

  3. Slack:

    This is a communicating business app that has made it easier for business owners to communicate with each other and various customers. Below are some of the benefits of using slack.
    • It helps members of an organization to share file in channels. Slack allows users to set channels to either a private or public conversation.
    • Slack allows you to make a voice call or video call with other members of your workspace. This makes various activities in a business done with ease.
    • You can also search for past conversation and relevant information from a team (member of your group) history.

  4. Podium:

    This is a business application that helps a business owner to get more online reviews.It is a messaging platform that connects you as a business owner to your customers. Apart from helping to get online reviews, podium can also be used for collecting payment

  5. Email app:

    One of the most efficient ways of communicating with customers is through an email. You don't need to be anxious of your personal or business account details or data been leaked because, the email app has a good security system. There are various email apps which you can use for your business. Some of which are: Microsoft Outlook,Proton mail, Dispatch and so on.
